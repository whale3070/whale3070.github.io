雅思口语课程-第十三课：To have的不同用法

“To have” 是第八个最频繁使用的词

1

To have的第一个意思就是 拥有
I have a pen.(美式英语)
I have got a pen.（英式英语）

2

To have的第二个意思就是 描述特征
I have a big nose.我有一个大鼻子
To have a bad temper有一个坏脾气

3

To have的第三个意思就是 描述活动
Have a shower
Have a rest
have a siester午睡
have a nap
have 40 winks

have a bite
have a taste
have a smell
have a think
have a ponder
have a try
have a got

语法错误: I have a breakfast。没有a
正确的说法： I have breakfast. I have lunch. I have dinner.

语法错误: I’ve got a black hair。没有a
正确的说法： I’ve got black hair我有黑色的头发

4

To have的第四个意思就是 描述人的性格
To have a lovely/ calm manner = speak in a calm and polite way
To have good manners = well behaved
To have bad manners = to be rude / impolite

5

To have的第五个意思就是 描述疾病

Have a cold 感冒了
Have a cough 咳嗽了
To be in the wars = to have many injuries or illness

6

To have time to kill = to have spare time
To have time on your hands = to have free time
To have a bone to pick with someone与某人有问题要解决 = have a reason to disagree with, or be angry with, someone
He doesn’t have a clue = He doesn’t know
To have a one track mind = to think about only one thing
To have the upper hand 占上风，占优势= to have an advantage over someone
To have money to burn = to have plenty of money to spend
To have a whale of a time 玩得很开心，过得很愉快 = to enjoy oneself a lot
To have a blast = to enjoy oneself a lot
