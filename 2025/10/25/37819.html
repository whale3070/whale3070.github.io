✅ 修改后正确版本（流畅自然）

This chart provides information about the average monthly changes in the prices of three metals — copper, nickel, and zinc — during 2014.

Overall, the prices of all three metals fluctuated throughout 2014, with nickel showing the most dramatic changes.

The price of nickel dropped sharply from January to June. It is worth noting that nickel recorded the highest price in January and the lowest in December. Between July and September, its price fell by around 1% and then remained stable.

The price of zinc was the second highest among the three metals in both January and December. However, between June and October, zinc showed the highest increase, peaking at about 1%.

From July to September, the prices of all three metals remained unchanged.

Overall, the prices of the three metals declined during the first half of the year, rose slightly in the middle months, and then stabilized for about three months. From September to December, the prices increased again, although nickel’s price remained stable at the end of the year.

🇨🇳 中文翻译（逐句）

这张图表提供了关于三种金属——铜、镍和锌——在2014年期间平均每月价格变动的信息。

总体来看，三种金属的价格在2014年全年都有波动，其中镍的变化最为剧烈。

镍的价格从1月到6月急剧下降。值得注意的是，镍的价格在1月达到最高点，而在12月降到最低点。从7月到9月，它的价格下降约1%，随后保持稳定。

锌的价格在1月和12月都位居三种金属中的第二位。然而，从6月到10月，锌的价格上升幅度最大，最高达到约1%。

从7月到9月，三种金属的价格都没有变化。

总体来说，三种金属的价格在上半年下降，在中间几个月略有上升，然后稳定了约三个月。从9月到12月，价格再次上升，但镍的价格在年底保持不变。

💡 改写讲解与常见错误说明

| 原句 | 问题 | 修改原因 |
| The price of three metals fluctuated during the 2014. | “the 2014”错误，不加冠词；“price of three metals”要复数 | → “The prices of all three metals fluctuated throughout 2014.” |
| The figure of nickel dropped dramatically… | “figure of nickel”不自然；应该用“price of nickel” | “figure”常指人数或统计数据，不适用于“价格” |
| decreased -1% | “decreased -1%”逻辑错误（下降本身就是负数） | → “fell by around 1%” |
| between July to September | “between”后要接“and” | → “between July and September” |
| The price of Zinc was the second in three metals… | 表达不自然，应该是“second highest among the three metals” | → 更地道、更学术 |
| which was peaked in 1%. | “was peaked”语法错误；“peak”是动词 | → “peaking at about 1%.” |
| all of metal price didn’t change at all. | 复数错误、语序不自然 | → “the prices of all three metals remained unchanged.” |
| the next of the month | 语义错误，应为“the following months” | 更自然的时间表达 |
| then keep stable | 时态错误（keep → kept / remained） | 一致使用过去时态 |
| three metal increased | 缺冠词与复数形式 | → “the three metals increased” |

🏅 Band 9 Model Answer

The chart illustrates the average monthly percentage changes in the prices of three metals — copper, nickel, and zinc — throughout 2014.
这张图表展示了 2014 年期间三种金属——铜、镍和锌——价格的平均月度百分比变化情况。

Overall, all three metals experienced price fluctuations during the year, though the extent of change varied considerably. Nickel underwent the most significant fall, while zinc showed moderate growth in the middle of the year before returning to its initial level by December.
**总体而言，**这三种金属在全年都经历了价格波动，不过波动的幅度差异相当明显。镍的价格出现了最显著的下跌，而锌在年中表现出温和的上涨趋势，随后在 12 月回落至最初水平。

At the beginning of 2014, nickel recorded the highest price level among the three metals. However, its value declined sharply from January to June, dropping by nearly 1%. After a brief period of stability between July and September, the price continued to decrease, reaching its lowest point in December. This indicates a steady downward trend over the entire year.
在 2014 年初，镍（nickel） 的价格在三种金属中处于最高水平。然而，从一月到六月，它的价格急剧下跌，下降了近 1%。在七月至九月间经历了短暂的稳定期后，价格继续下滑，并在十二月达到最低点。这表明镍在全年都呈现出一种持续下降的趋势。

By contrast, the price of zinc exhibited a milder pattern of change. Although it started the year in second place, zinc’s price rose gradually from June onwards, peaking at around 1% in September — the highest value recorded by any of the metals during the year — before falling slightly towards December.
相比之下，锌（zinc） 的价格变化幅度较小。虽然它在年初位居第二，但从六月开始，锌价逐渐上升，并在九月达到约 1% 的峰值——这是全年三种金属中最高的价格水平——随后在接近十二月时略有回落。

The price of copper, meanwhile, remained the most stable throughout the period, showing only minor variations. It is also worth noting that from July to September, all three metals displayed virtually no change, reflecting a brief phase of market stability.
与此同时，铜（copper） 的价格在整个时期内保持最为稳定，只出现了轻微的波动。值得注意的是，从七月到九月，这三种金属的价格几乎没有任何变化，这反映出当时市场曾经历过一段短暂的稳定期。

In summary, while the first half of the year was characterized by a decline across all three metals, the latter half saw a modest recovery, particularly for zinc. Overall, nickel suffered the most pronounced fall, whereas copper was least affected by market fluctuations.
总的来说，虽然上半年三种金属的价格都呈下降趋势，但下半年出现了小幅回升，尤其是锌的表现最为明显。总体而言，镍的跌幅最为显著，而铜受市场波动的影响最小。

✅ Band 9 特点讲解：

| 项目 | Band 9 范文亮点 | 示例 |
| 句式多样化 | 倒装、让步句、对比句 | “By contrast, the price of zinc exhibited a milder pattern of change.” |
| 学术词汇 | 代替简单动词 | “underwent”, “recorded”, “exhibited”, “characterized by”, “fluctuations” |
| 逻辑清晰 | 时间顺序 + 对比结构 | 先讲总体趋势，再分金属分析，最后总结 |
| 自然衔接词 | 高级连接词 | “However”, “By contrast”, “Meanwhile”, “It is also worth noting that” |
| 语气自然流畅 | 符合雅思写作语体 | 没有口语化表达（如“the number was…”） |
